The Philippines is an archipelagic country located in Southeast Asia. It consists of 7641 islands, which makes it the second-largest archipelago in the world.

Listed here are six landmarks that I personally love:

Puerto Princesa Underground River, Palawan

-gateway to the Philippines’ Last Frontier
-world’s longest navigable underground river
-UNESCO World Heritage Site

White Beach, Boracay

-4-kilometer stretch of pure white, powdery sand
-"postcard-perfect"

Banaue Rice Terraces, Ifugao

-A UNESCO World Heritage Site
-It offers spectacular views of emerald hues
-rice-growing seasons are April-May and October-November
